# .edu account on Azure

Activate a Microsoft Azure account using your .edu email and claim student credit without a card.

[toc]

## Requirements

- Active .edu institutional email
- Inbox access for verification

## Steps

1. Go to Azure for Students
2. Sign up with your .edu email
3. Verify identity and complete profile
4. Claim the promotional credit

## Resources

- Azure for Students: https://azure.microsoft.com/free/students/
